We care about you and your Family

OTP is a premier paediatric center established by Dr. Shagufta Yousaf. Our aim is to provide Complete, Compassionate and High quality healthcreate to children and adolescents.












Dr. Shagufta Yousaf

– a native of the South, moved to Tucson in 1972 and was a community pediatrician here until 2001 when she moved to northern Arizona and founded White Mountain Pediatrics. Wanting to be closer to her grandchildren, Dr. Shagufta returned to Tucson in 2012, and joined Children’s Clinics as a pediatrician in the Primary Care unit.









Primary Care

All children, from birth to adulthood, are welcome at the Olive Tree Paediatrics under the highly trained and experienced care of our primary care Physician Dr. Shagufta Yousaf.
